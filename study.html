Numbers

1-10

1 = Echad

2 = Shtaim

3 = Shalosh

4 = Arba

5 = Hamesh

6 = Shesh

7 = Sheva

8 = Shmoneh

9 = Tesha

10 = Eser

11-19

11 = Echad esre

12 = Shtaim esre

13 = Shalosh esre

14 = Arba esre

15 = Hamesh esre

16 = Shesh esre

17 = Sheva esre

18 = Shmoneh esre

19 = Tesha esre

20-100

20 = Esrim

30 = Shloshim

40 = Arbaim

50 = Hamishim

60 = Sheshim

70 = Shivim

80 = Shmonim

90 = Tishim

100 = Mea

To count a two-digit number above 20, remember to say the number followed by a v' and then the number. So 21 would be esrim v'echad, 45 would be arbaim v'hamesh, and 83 would be shmonim v'shalosh.
