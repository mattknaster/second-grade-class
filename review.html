Numbers

If you click on the word, a new window will open up with the pronunciation.

1-10

1 = Echad

2 = Shtaim

3 = Shalosh

4 = Arba

5 = Hamesh

6 = Shesh

7 = Sheva

8 = Shmoneh

9 = Tesha

10 = Eser

11-19

11 = Echad esre

12 = Shtaim esre

13 = Shalosh esre

14 = Arba esre

15 = Hamesh esre

16 = Shesh esre

17 = Sheva esre

18 = Shmoneh esre

19 = Tesha esre

20-100

20 = Esrim

30 = Shloshim

40 = Arbaim

50 = Hamishim

60 = Sheshim

70 = Shivim

80 = Shmonim

90 = Tishim

100 = Mea

To count from 21-99, remember to say the number in the 20-100 column first, then add a v', and then say the number in the 1-10 column. So 41 would be arbaim v'echad. 67 would be sheshim v'sheva. 92 would be tishim v'shtaim.
